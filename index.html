Hey, I'm Marco, a PhD candidate and research associate working in digital mental health. Currently I'm a visiting research scholar at Stanford in the Division of Interdisciplinary Brain Sciences within the Department of Psychiatry and Behavioral Sciences. I'm interested in digital solutions that improve prevention and treatment outcomes for children, adolescents, and families facing developmental and psychosocial challenges. My work focuses on improving access to mental health care for underserved populations, such as refugee families, through multilingual digital interventions that complement parenting programs and psychosocial support.
