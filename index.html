Marco Gassner

Hey, I'm Marco a PhD candidate and research associate working in the field of digital mental health. I'm interested in digital mental health solutions to improve prevention and treatment outcomes for children and adolescents facing developmental risks and mental health challenges. My research focuses on digital mental health and early support for children and families facing developmental and psychosocial challenges. I’m particularly interested in how digital tools can make mental health care more accessible for underserved populations, such as refugee families. My work involves developing and studying multilingual digital interventions that complement parenting programs and psychosocial support, with the goal of improving prevention and treatment outcomes.
